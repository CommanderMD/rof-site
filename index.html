The Record

Officially Amazing.

On August 13, 2019, in Bartlett, Tennessee, Issac D. Coleman performed 51 thumb push-ups in a single minute — becoming the first and only American to hold the Guinness World Record for the feat.

He didn't do it for a trophy. Every record attempt doubles as a fundraiser for St. Jude Children's Research Hospital, under his movement: "United Against Cancer Is the Answer."
